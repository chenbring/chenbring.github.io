Personal Profile

Bingyang Chen, Ph.D., born 1993, is a young faculty member at Henan University of Technology.

His research focuses on physics-constrained deep learning modeling and battery health management. He has led multiple national and provincial research projects, including the National Natural Science Foundation of China, Henan Science and Technology Key Projects, university-level open projects, high-level talent projects, and innovation funds.

He has published over 20 papers in reputable journals and conferences, including 10 papers as first/corresponding author in top-tier journals (Q1, Chinese Academy of Sciences) and 2 papers in Q2 journals. He has filed 4 invention patents, with 1 already granted. He conducted a government-sponsored visiting study in the Computer Science Department at the University of Toronto, Canada. His awards include the National Scholarship for Ph.D. students and the Golden Bridge Award from the China Technology Market Association, among others.

He has mentored multiple students who won first prizes in national competitions, received National Scholarships and National Encouragement Scholarships, and successfully secured graduate admissions to top Chinese universities such as the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China and Southeast University.

研究方向

  1. 基于领域知识的数据表征方法：结合领域知识，针对时序传感数据，图像数据，建立多模态表征方法。

Research Interests

  1. Domain knowledge-based data representation: Develop multimodal representation methods for time-series sensor data and image data by integrating domain knowledge.
  2. Sample-constrained adaptive modeling: Build domain-specific large language models through collaborative/edge computing or data alignment strategies to improve performance in few-shot tasks.
